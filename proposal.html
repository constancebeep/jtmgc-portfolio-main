Common Gaps

Small details can quietly cost you inquiries.

These are the issues I often look for first because they are fixable, practical, and directly tied to how customers judge a business online.

Incomplete Google Business Profile

Missing services, categories, photos, descriptions, hours, or updates can make a business look less active.

Inconsistent listings

Different names, addresses, or phone numbers across directories can weaken trust and local search consistency.

Low local search clarity

Customers may not immediately see the services, locations, or areas your business supports.

No regular updates

Inactive websites and social profiles can make a business feel quiet, even when the team is busy offline.

No competitor awareness

It helps to understand what nearby competitors are doing and where your business can stand out.

No reporting rhythm

Without simple tracking, it is hard to know what is improving and what needs a different approach.
